Our Story

Born from passion,
baked with heart

Cake Club Colombo is a home-based artisan bakery nestled in the heart of Colombo, Sri Lanka. We believe every celebration deserves a cake as unique as the person you're celebrating — crafted with premium ingredients, obsessive attention to detail, and a whole lot of love.

From intimate birthday cakes to grand wedding centrepieces, each creation is fully customised to your vision. Follow our journey on Instagram @cakeclubcolombo for daily sweet inspiration.

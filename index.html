Our Story

A cake shop with a
point of view

Cake Club Colombo is a Colombo-based home bakery specialising in minimal, character-driven cakes that say exactly what you want them to say — without saying too much. Less clutter, more you.

We do custom bento cakes (the adorable single-serve Korean-style boxes) as well as regular cakes in sizes from 500g to 2kg. Every cake is made to order, so it's always fresh and always yours.

Bento Cakes

Our signature: adorable single-serve mini cakes in cute white boxes. Minimalist, personalised, and full of personality. Perfect for gifting.

  • Single serve, boxed
  • Custom message & design
  • Themed options (e.g. Spider-Man, florals)
  • Pearl borders, bows, minimal piping
🎂

Regular Cakes

Full-sized custom cakes for birthdays, anniversaries and special occasions. Minimal designs with maximum impact.

  • Sizes: 500g, 1kg, 2kg
  • Custom flavours & designs
  • Buttercream & fondant finishes
  • Aurudu & seasonal specials

Chocolate Collection

Our viral chocolate range — rich ganache drip cakes loaded with toppings. Available as bento or regular sizes.

  • Customisable toppings
